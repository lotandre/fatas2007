{% blocktrans %}This experiment has three rounds.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Each round lasts a variable number of periods (from 1 to 15). The exact length of each round depends on a random draw.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}At the beginning of each round, you will get a Card Deck with 14 green cards and 1 red card.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}At the end of each period, one card is randomly drawn out of the Card Deck.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}If the card drawn is green, you will proceed to the next period, and the card will be removed. If the card draw is red, the round will end.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}At the beginning of each round, you must decide in which period you want to Start earning Payoffs (in points).{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}The later your Start period, the more you will earn per period as shown in the table below.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}You will continue earning the same number of points as in the Start period until you draw a red card. In this case, the round ends. If you draw the red card before your Start period, you will earn zero.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Example: Suppose that at the beginning of a round you chose to Start receiving a Payoff on Period 8. Suppose also that you draw green cards until Period 9 and at the end of Period 10 you draw a red card. In this case, you would earn zero in Period 1 to 7 (because you chose to Start receiving Payoffs in Period 8). In period 8, you earn 42 points (see table above). In period 9 and 10, while you are still active, you continue earning 42 points. In Period 11 you earn zero, since at the end of Period 10 you draw the red card and the round ended.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}We sum your Total Payoff points at the end of each round.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}The computer will randomly select one of the 3 rounds. The Total Payoff points in that round will be exchanged for Euros at a rate of € 1.00 = 23 points.{% endblocktrans %}
